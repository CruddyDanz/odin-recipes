Anchovy Pizza

How could you go wrong with pizza mate?

Ingredients:

  • 4 anchovy fillets
  • half fresh red chilli, sliced
  • extra virgin olive oil
  • half lemon, zest and juice of
  • 3 tablespoons tomato sauce
  • 50g mozarella
  • 1 tablespoon of parsley
  • sea salt and pepper

Steps:

  1. Cut the fillets in half and add to a bowl of sliced chilli, couple of spoons of virgin olive oil and the lemon zest
  2. Squeeze a little bit of lemon juice onto the mix and mix'em up
  3. Let the anchovies sit in the marinade for 15 minutes
  4. Smeer the tomato sauce evenly over the pizza base and scatter torn pieces of mozarella
  5. Evenly lay over the anchovy fillets, scatter over the chilli and sprinkle the parsley and seasoning
  6. Cook until golden and crisp
